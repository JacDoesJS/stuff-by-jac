Luxury Handwoven Goods

I delight in the creative process. Magpie-like, I collect plant-fibre yarns and combine them to make unique and texturally exciting cloth, celebrating my love of colour. I then sew this fabric into all kinds of decorative items.

My background in music composition and performance, my love of complex knitting patterns and developing understanding of weaving drafts have all contributed to my desire to become a web developer. All of these activities stimulate my brain which loves to, organise, create, and problem solve.

I owe a debt of gratitude to my Irish granny who it seemed, out of necessity, could literally make something from seemingly not much at all. I enjoy trying to make the most of whatever resources I have.
